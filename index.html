Proposed approach

A 90-day plan to establish credibility, build foundations, and accelerate proposal velocity.

Days 1–30 · Audit and inventory

Learn the buyer, the deals, and the assets

  • Audit the existing proposal response library; identify content gaps, outdated assets, and brand inconsistencies
  • Shadow sales conversations to understand the healthcare logistics buyer journey, common objections, and win patterns
  • Map the current proposal workflow end-to-end and document hand-off points between marketing, sales, and SMEs

Days 31–60 · Voice of sales

Build modular content driven by what reps actually need

  • Establish quarterly Voice-of-Sales calls (model proven at Life Fitness) to surface competitive intelligence and content gaps
  • Design modular, brand-aligned content blocks that compress RFP turnaround without sacrificing quality
  • Stand up a presentation template system aligned to MedSpeed's brand: customizable per prospect, consistent in voice

Days 61–90 · Velocity and intelligence

Systematize feedback loops and measure what matters

  • Lead the cross-functional task force on market intelligence; coordinate reporting cadence and distribution
  • Implement a win/loss feedback loop that feeds deal outcomes back into the proposal content library
  • Establish baseline metrics for proposal cycle time, win rate, and content reuse to enable ongoing improvement

Relevant case studies

Case 5.1

Mill Creek key account RFP narrative

SituationLife Fitness was pursuing a $700K+/year strategic engagement with a major Multi-Unit Housing developer. The opportunity required a presentation that went beyond product features to position Life Fitness as a strategic partner.
ApproachSynthesized third-party research from McKinsey, Global Wellness Institute, Murphy Research, and ACSM into a consultative narrative covering wellness real estate trends, resident demographics, strength training growth, layout philosophy, and ADA compliance.
ResultThe presentation set the stage for all subsequent design, technology, and product sessions in the engagement and directly informed downstream campaign and account strategy.

Case 5.2

MUH warranty promotional campaign

SituationMulti-Unit Housing was an underperforming segment with a large database of dormant contacts and low engagement on standard marketing sends.
ApproachBuilt a targeted campaign in Pardot Engagement Studios, sourced 8,000+ contacts via Salesforce and ZoomInfo enrichment, and partnered with three inside sellers to deliver personalized outreach from their individual accounts rather than generic marketing sends.
Result34 new opportunities and $670K+ in attributed pipeline from a single campaign.

Case 5.3

Working with US Marketing playbook

SituationField sellers at Life Fitness lacked a clear roadmap for engaging the marketing team — what existed, what marketing could provide, and how to request support.
ApproachAuthored the "Working with US Marketing" guide from scratch, covering available collateral, customer insight resources, regional event support, paid media, and FAQs, with a creative asset inspiration board to showcase breadth.
ResultFirst document of its kind in the segment; reduced friction between marketing and sales and accelerated asset request cycles.
